üå± Our Story

Mind Bloom was founded in 2023 by a group of university students who recognized the critical lack of accessible and non-judgmental mental health support for their peers. What started as a small campus initiative quickly grew into a nation-wide campaign driven by the desire to normalize conversations around mental health challenges.

  • Create safe, judgment-free zones in local schools.
